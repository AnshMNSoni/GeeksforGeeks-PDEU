Welcome to the GeeksforGeeks Student Chapter at PDEU—

where caffeine, code, and creativity collide! We're a bunch of tech enthusiasts on a mission to make learning fun, practical, and future-ready.

Whether you’re a coding ninja or just figuring out what Git really means (spoiler: it's not an insult), we've got something for you! From CS fundamentals to hot topics like Web Dev, ML, App Dev, and even Cloud Computing, we’re here to turn confusion into clarity—and maybe a few late-night debugging sessions.

Workshops, coding competitions, and meme-worthy community vibes? Check. A place to learn, grow, and connect with people who get just as excited about semicolons and syntax? Double-check. So, if you’re ready to level up your skills and have some fun while doing it, welcome to the geek side—we’ve got code!
